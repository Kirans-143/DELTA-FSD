Spider-Man

About Spider man

Spider-Man is a is a american superhero published by Marvel Comics . Created by writer-editor Stan lee and artist Steve ditko, he first appeared in the anthology comic book Amazing Fantasy #15(Aug 1962) in the Silver age comic book. He has been featured in comic books, television shows, films, video games, novels, and plays
